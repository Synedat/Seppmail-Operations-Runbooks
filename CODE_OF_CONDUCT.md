# Code of Conduct

This repository is maintained by **Synedat Group GmbH** and positioned as a partner-oriented public engineering repository in the SEPPmail ecosystem.

## Our standard

Participate in a professional, respectful and solution-focused way. We welcome technical feedback, issue reports, improvements, field experience and documentation contributions.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on facts, evidence and reproducible observations.
- Keep security-sensitive information out of public threads.
- Use responsible disclosure for vulnerabilities.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination or intimidation.
- Publishing secrets, credentials, customer data or attack details.
- Misrepresentation of vendor support, certification status or compliance claims.

## Reporting

For conduct or moderation concerns, use the contact paths documented in [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md) and [docs/SECURITY-DISCLOSURE.md](docs/SECURITY-DISCLOSURE.md).
