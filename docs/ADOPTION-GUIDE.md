# Adoption guide

## 1. classify the target environment

Identify whether the target is lab, pilot, production-preparation or production.

## 2. agree on the operating model

Confirm who owns architecture, who executes changes, who approves them and who reviews evidence.

## 3. adapt the examples

Replace placeholders, URLs, tenant identifiers and naming standards. Remove sample data that is not needed.

## 4. test in a safe environment

Execute the examples in a non-production environment first and validate rollback assumptions.

## 5. document the local variant

Preserve customer-specific assumptions in architecture notes, ADRs or runbooks.

## 6. operationalise

Decide which checks become scheduled jobs, CI validations or manual runbook steps.
