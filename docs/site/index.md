# Seppmail-Operations-Runbooks

Structured runbooks for operating, troubleshooting and rehearsing SEPPmail-adjacent messaging environments.

## Why this repository exists

Use this repository to turn tribal knowledge into concise operational procedures and rehearsal material.

## What you will find here

- Architecture and implementation notes
- Security, compliance and control mapping content
- Example scripts and reusable patterns
- Operations, troubleshooting and observability guidance
- Demo scenarios, release notes and community entry points

## Audience

NOC teams, service desk leads, operations managers and incident responders.

## Focus areas

incident handling, maintenance choreography, recovery drills, evidence capture and team handover
